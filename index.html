The Reality

What Happens When
Your Beckham Law Expires?

01

Tax Shock

Your flat 24% rate disappears. Worldwide income becomes taxable at progressive rates up to 47%. Every dividend, every gain, every foreign rental — all taxed.

02

Wealth Tax Exposure

Global assets suddenly fall under Spain's Wealth Tax and the new Solidarity Tax on large fortunes. Your portfolio, your holdings — all in scope.

03

The Exit Tax Trap

Considering leaving Spain? Unrealised capital gains may be taxed on departure. You're locked in — or you pay to leave.

04

Succession Complexity

Assets spread across jurisdictions mean multiple probate processes, conflicting inheritance laws, and costly international proceedings.

05

Unrealised Gains at Risk

Shares, portfolios, company stakes that have appreciated in value — gains you haven't even cashed in — become taxable events.

06

No Plan for "After"

Most expats focused on the move to Spain. Few planned for what happens when the tax shield drops. The clock is ticking.

The Solution

One Structure. Complete Protection.

The Luxembourg Unit Linked is a proven, regulated insurance wrapper that serves as a powerful wealth planning tool across your entire lifecycle — from accumulation through protection to intergenerational transfer.

I

Indefinite Tax Deferral

All returns — dividends, interest, capital gains — compound tax-free inside the structure. You choose when and how much to withdraw, and only pay tax on the proportional gain at that moment.

II

Exit Tax Avoidance

Relocating from Spain? Unrealised gains inside the Unit Linked are not subject to exit taxation, giving you full freedom to move without fiscal penalty.

III

Wealth & Solidarity Tax Reduction

Using the Unit Linked as collateral for a Lombard credit line, you can invest in tax-exempt assets and reduce your Wealth Tax and Solidarity Tax burden — without liquidating your portfolio.

IV

Tax-Efficient Donations & Succession

Donate assets without triggering capital gains tax in the IRPF. Condition inheritance payouts to allow beneficiaries time to relocate to more favourable jurisdictions.

V

Asset Protection & Privacy

Assets within the Unit Linked benefit from legal protection against seizure. No public register lists policyholders, ensuring complete confidentiality of your financial affairs.

VI

Cross-Border Portability

Moving to Portugal, Italy, or beyond? The structure travels with you — maintaining its tax efficiency across jurisdictions without creating taxable events.
